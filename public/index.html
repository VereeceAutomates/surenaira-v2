How Cross-Market Arbs Work

A cross-market arb uses two different market types that together cover 100% of all outcomes.

Example: Home (-1:0) HDP × Away Win:

Leg A: Home (-1:0) EH
 → Wins if: Home 2-0+, or 1-0, or Draw
Leg B: Away Win (1X2)
 → Wins if: Away wins
Together = all results covered ✓

Implied = (1/oddsA) + (1/oddsB)
Profit% = (1 − implied) / implied × 100
